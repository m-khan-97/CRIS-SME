# Transformation Strategy

## North Star

CRIS-SME should become a state-of-the-art evidence-driven cloud risk decision methodology for SMEs.

The product promise is simple:

> Evidence in. Decisions out.

CRIS-SME turns cloud posture evidence into defensible risk decisions, governance memory, assurance artifacts, and remediation intelligence that lean teams can actually execute.

## Category Direction

CRIS-SME should not position itself as another scanner. The stronger category is:

**Cloud Risk Governance Automation for SMEs**

This places CRIS-SME between three existing markets:

- CSPM/CNAPP tools that collect cloud posture but often produce too much alert volume for SMEs
- GRC tools that track controls but are usually disconnected from live cloud evidence
- cyber-insurance questionnaires that demand proof but rarely give practical remediation intelligence

CRIS-SME combines evidence collection, deterministic control decisions, lifecycle governance, compliance translation, insurance readiness, and board-ready reporting in one SME-shaped workflow.

## Users And Beneficiaries

The immediate work is technical transformation, but the methodology should remain grounded in real users.

### SME Owner Or Operations Lead

Needs a plain answer to: "What cloud risks matter this month, what should we fix first, and what can we prove to stakeholders?"

CRIS-SME value:

- prioritizes risk into practical action
- avoids expensive enterprise platform overhead
- produces board and insurer-ready outputs
- supports local/private execution

### Lean Technical Team

Needs a repeatable way to turn Azure evidence into controls, findings, ownership, and remediation planning.

CRIS-SME value:

- deterministic findings with evidence lineage
- confidence and observability boundaries
- exception lifecycle with expiry
- drift and recurring regression visibility

### MSP Or Virtual CISO

Needs repeatable assessments and reports across many small clients.

CRIS-SME value:

- multi-tenant portfolio view
- standardized evidence packs
- white-label executive and insurance exports
- exception and action tracking across clients

### Insurer Or Assurance Partner

Needs answers backed by current, explainable technical evidence.

CRIS-SME value:

- mapped questionnaire answers
- evidence sufficiency labels
- confidence calibration
- signed report manifests

## Differentiation

CRIS-SME should compete on decision quality, not scanner breadth.

Against enterprise CNAPP/CSPM:

- lighter operating model
- lower cost
- clearer evidence boundaries
- SME-specific remediation and reporting

Against open-source scanners:

- lifecycle-aware findings
- confidence calibration
- exceptions and accepted risk
- insurance and executive artifacts
- deterministic score breakdowns

Against GRC tools:

- live cloud evidence
- evidence lineage
- drift detection
- technical-to-business translation

## Product Pillars

### 1. Deterministic Decision Core

Scores, priorities, confidence, and compliance interpretation remain formula-based and versioned.

### 2. Evidence Honesty

Unavailable evidence is recorded as unavailable, not silently treated as safe.

### 3. Lifecycle Governance

Findings move through ownership, action, accepted risk, suppression, expiry, and resolution.

### 4. Assurance-Ready Outputs

Every assessment can produce board, insurer, auditor, technical, benchmark, and appendix artifacts.

### 5. SME Operating Reality

Remediation must account for time, budget, skill, and sequence.

### 6. AI As Narrator, Never Authority

AI may explain and draft, but the deterministic report remains authoritative.

## Packaging

### Community

Local mock/Azure runs, static reports, sample policy packs, and limited dashboard publishing.

### SME Starter

One cloud account, scheduled assessments, history, executive report, and core remediation plan.

### SME Pro

Exceptions, insurance pack, API access, advanced drift, remediation simulation, and signed reports.

### MSP

Multi-tenant portfolio console, client-ready exports, white-label reports, and recurring regression tracking.

### Assurance Add-On

Insurer questionnaire mapping, audit appendix, evidence sufficiency report, and report integrity manifest.

## Pricing Direction

Pricing should map to avoided consultant effort and improved assurance readiness, not raw scan count.

- Community: free
- SME Starter: 49-99 GBP/month
- SME Pro: 199-399 GBP/month
- MSP: 799+ GBP/month
- Assurance Add-On: fixed pack fee or annual add-on

## Innovation Narrative

SMEs are increasingly asked to prove cloud governance to customers, insurers, boards, and regulators, but most tools either overwhelm them with alerts or require enterprise security teams.

CRIS-SME solves the proof gap: it turns cloud evidence into defensible decisions, practical remediation, and stakeholder-ready assurance artifacts.

The long-term system can expand from Azure-first assessment into a multi-cloud risk decision API, MSP console, insurance evidence gateway, and control-pack marketplace. That commercial direction is secondary to the current R&D priority: proving the methodology is novel, useful, and technically credible.
